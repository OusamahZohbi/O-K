Our Story

Every great love story deserves to be written down. Here is ours.

The Beginning

The Day Everything Changed

We first saw each other at Belmore while attending classes. I was on my motorbike and you noticed me. Not long after, we properly met and sat down to talk. From that very first conversation, I knew straight away — you were the one. The way you laughed, the way you spoke, your beauty and your character… everything felt right. Alhamdulillah, Allah brought us together.

Getting Closer

When I Knew

Our connection grew quickly after we met. I felt more and more sure with every conversation that you were the woman I wanted to marry, insha’Allah. Alhamdulillah, things moved smoothly and we got married shortly after.

A Big Step

We Made It Official

Our nikah was small and intimate — just our close families and the people we love. It was simple, full of barakah, and one of the happiest days of my life.

The early days of marriage brought some tough times, but we turned to Allah, supported each other, and got through them. Alhamdulillah, those challenges only made our bond stronger.

Adventures Together

Life Side by Side

After some time, we moved into our own place. Having our own home was such a blessing. Waking up next to you, falling asleep beside you, and laughing together every day — these simple moments felt like pure gifts from Allah.

One beautiful evening after we were married, we went for a walk along the beach at sunset. We climbed some rocks and sat together watching the ocean. It was peaceful and special — a moment I will always remember.

Another huge blessing was going for Hajj together. We performed tawaf, sat side by side in Arafah making dua, did Saee together, and worshipped as husband and wife. There were even funny moments, like when you elbowed me in the face at the hotel and we I almost had to be rushed to the hospital. The bufettttttt was soooo good, the waffles and the juice and everything. That whole journey brought us so much closer to Allah and to each other. May Allah accept our Hajj and keep us steadfast upon His religion, Ameen.

Today & Always

Here, on This Eid

Now, this blessed Eid, I look at us and my heart is full of gratitude. Alhamdulillah, we are in the best stage of our marriage so far. We’ve matured, we resolve issues quickly, we communicate better, and we truly love spending time with each other more than ever. Allahumabarik.

You have been my biggest support and my greatest blessing after Islam. I thank Allah every single day for you, Klementina. I love you for the sake of Allah and I make dua that He keeps our hearts close in this life and grants us Jannah together. Ameen.
