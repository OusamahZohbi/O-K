Why I Love You

Klementina, here are just a few of the infinite reasons you have my whole heart.

01

Your Character

From the very first time we sat and talked, your character stood out to me. Your good manners, the way you carry yourself with dignity, and how real you are — it all made me think straight away, “This is the kind of woman I want to marry.” Alhamdulillah, Allah blessed me with someone whose akhlaq is so strong and beautiful.

02

Your Selflessness

You are so selfless. You always think of others before yourself — whether it’s family, friends, or me. Even when you’re tired or things are hard, you still put people first. That quality in you is rare, and I see it every single day. It makes me love and respect you even more.

03

How Much You Care for Me

You care for me so much — more than I can ever put into words. The way you check on me, look after me, and make sure I’m okay in every way… it touches my heart deeply. I feel truly loved and looked after because of you. These acts of care bring so much barakah into our home.

04

Your Loyalty and Strength

Through everything we’ve been through and everything I’ve done, you’ve stayed right by my side. You never gave up on us. You kept turning to Allah and kept supporting me even when things were difficult. You are truly amazing, Allahumabarik. I don’t take that for granted — not even for one day.

05

How You Make Me Better

Being married to you has changed me for the better, alhamdulillah. You’ve helped me become more patient, more grateful, and a better Muslim and husband. You push me gently towards good, and I’ve grown so much because you’re in my life.

06

Your Laugh

Your laugh is one of my favourite sounds. When something makes you laugh, it’s genuine and it lights up everything around you. Even on hard days, hearing it makes me smile and reminds me how blessed I am.

07

Your Silly Side

I love the silly little things you do sometimes. The random funny faces, the unexpected jokes, or the way you make me laugh at the smallest things. Those moments remind me not to take life too seriously and make our days so much brighter.

08

The Peace You Bring

After everything we’ve been through, coming home to you brings me real peace. You’ve become my sakinah — the calm in our life — and being with you makes me feel safe and content. I thank Allah every day for this mercy between us.

09

Your Honesty and Maturity

I really appreciate how honest you are with me. We’ve learned to communicate better and resolve issues much quicker now, and that maturity has brought so much barakah into our marriage. It makes me feel like we’re true partners for this life and the next.

10

Simply, You

Above all, I love you just because you are you — Klementina. Not for what you do, but for who you are: my wife, my companion, and one of the greatest blessings Allah has given me after Islam. That is more than enough.

"The list goes on forever, Nanoosti."

— Ousamah 🌸
